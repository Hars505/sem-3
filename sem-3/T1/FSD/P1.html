A paragraph is a self-contained unit of writing focused on a single main idea, composed of several related sentences that support that idea.
It typically begins with an indentation or by skipping a line, and contains a topic sentence,
supporting sentences with evidence and explanation, and sometimes a concluding sentence to transition to the next topic.
Paragraphs help organize longer pieces of writing, making them easier for readers to understand the author's thoughts and the overall structure of the text
